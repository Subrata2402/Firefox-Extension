Chegg Question Notifier

How to use extension:

  1. Go to Expert Q&A page.
  2. You will see a line orange paragraph after "Hello, Expert!", refresh the page if in case you don't.
  3. That's it! you will be notified when a question is available.

Make sure:

  1. You have decent internet speed.
  2. And the chrome notifications are turned on.
